How We Make Money

Integration Directory is free to use and will always remain free. We sustain the site through:

  • Affiliate commissions: When you sign up for tools we recommend (like Make.com), we may earn a small commission at no cost to you.
  • Display advertising: We display relevant ads via Google AdSense.
  • Sponsored placements: Occasionally, tools may pay to be featured. We always disclose these clearly.

We only recommend tools we genuinely believe in. Our editorial recommendations are never influenced by payments.
