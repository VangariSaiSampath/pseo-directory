6. Cookies Policy

We use cookies — small text files stored on your device — to operate the site, understand how it is used, and serve relevant advertising. Below is a complete list of every cookie category we use, what each one does, and how long it lasts.

Cookie table

Cookie / name Set by Purpose Duration Category
_ga, _ga_* Google Analytics Counts unique visitors and tracks which pages are viewed, so we can understand site usage and improve content. 2 years Analytics
_gid Google Analytics Distinguishes users within a 24-hour window. Used alongside _ga to count sessions accurately. 24 hours Analytics
_gat Google Analytics Throttles the rate of requests to Google Analytics servers to prevent overloading. 1 minute Analytics
__gads, __gpi Google AdSense Records which ads you have already been shown to prevent the same ad appearing too often (frequency capping). Also used for ad performance measurement. 13 months Advertising
NID, DSID, IDE Google (DoubleClick) Builds a profile of your browsing interests to show personalised ads across Google partner sites. You can opt out at any time (see below). 6–13 months Advertising
session Integration Directory Keeps your search session active so forms and search features work correctly during your visit. Session only (deleted when you close your browser) Essential
Affiliate cookies Make.com, Flipkart, and other affiliate partners Set when you click an affiliate link. Records the referral so we are credited if you sign up or make a purchase. No personal data is shared with us by these partners. 30–90 days (varies by partner) Affiliate / Functional

Chrome · Firefox · Safari · Edge
